How it works

Four steps, same order every real CBT follows

No accounts, no onboarding forms — pick a section and the clock starts, exactly like the exam server does on test day.

01

Choose a section

Quant, Reasoning, English, Awareness or Computer Knowledge — practice one at a time or mix a full set.

02

Clock starts

A live countdown pins to the top bar. It doesn't pause when you switch questions — same as the real terminal.

03

Select an option, mark a question for review, or skip it. The palette on the right tracks every state.

04

Submit & review

Get your score instantly, with a question-by-question breakdown of what you got right and why.
